Jalapeño Poppers

Description

This homemade Jalapeño Poppers recipe was made by taking the best features of three or four other popper recipes and combining them to make something that tastes wonderful. Adjust the amount of bacon bits and type of shredded cheese to taste. Lots of tests will help you figure out the best way you like your Jalapeño poppers.

Jalapeño poppers: They're warm, spicy, cheesy, and ultra-indulgent. What more could you ask for in an appetizer? These homemade Jalapeño poppers taste like they're from a restaurant, but they're quite simple to make in the comfort of your own kitchen.
